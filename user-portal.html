Safety Guidelines

If You Encounter Dangerous Wildlife

  • Stay calm and do not run
  • Back away slowly while facing the animal
  • Make yourself appear larger by raising your arms
  • Do not make direct eye contact with predators
  • Use the SOS button immediately for emergency help
  • Make noise to alert others in the area

Using the SOS System

  • Press the SOS button in bottom-right corner
  • Your location is automatically shared
  • Forest authorities are notified immediately
  • Follow escape route guidance on screen
  • Stay on the line if contacted by authorities
  • Move to nearest safe zone as indicated

Agricultural Protection Tips

  • Install proper fencing around crop areas
  • Use motion-activated lights as deterrents
  • Harvest crops promptly to reduce attraction
  • Store produce securely away from forest edges
  • Report crop damage to authorities
  • Coordinate with neighbors for community watch

Community Safety Measures

  • Travel in groups during high-risk times
  • Avoid forest areas at dawn and dusk
  • Keep children supervised near forest borders
  • Secure livestock in protected enclosures
  • Share sightings with community members
  • Participate in awareness programs
